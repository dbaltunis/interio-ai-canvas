## Getting Started

### Creating Your Store

1. Navigate to the **Online Store** tab in your InterioApp dashboard
2. Click **"Launch Online Store"**
3. Enter your store name
4. Select a template that matches your brand style:
   - **Classic Elegance** - Timeless design with sophisticated typography
   - **Modern Minimalist** - Clean lines and contemporary aesthetics
   - **Bold Professional** - Strong visual presence and professional look

Your store will be automatically populated with:
- Default pages (Home, Products, About, Contact)
- Your existing active inventory items
- Your business branding (logo, colors)
- Pre-configured SEO settings

## Store Dashboard

The Store Dashboard provides an overview of your store's performance:

### Key Metrics
- **New Inquiries**: Customer contact requests from the past 7 days
- **Quote Requests**: Total active quote requests from customers
- **Products Online**: Number of visible products in your store

### Quick Actions
- **View Store**: Open your public storefront in a new tab
- **Edit Pages**: Customize your store's content and layout
- **Manage Products**: Control which products are visible and featured
- **Store Settings**: Configure branding, SEO, domain, and payments

### Publishing
- Toggle between **Published** and **Unpublished** states
- Unpublishing hides your store from public view
- Your store URL: `/store/[your-store-slug]`

## Product Management

### Product Visibility

Control which inventory items appear in your online store:

1. Go to **Manage Products** from the Store Dashboard
2. Toggle product visibility with the **eye icon**
3. Mark products as **featured** to highlight them on your homepage
4. Reorder products using **drag-and-drop**

### Product Customization

For each product, you can:
- Add custom descriptions specific to your online store
- Upload additional product images
- Set product order/priority
- Mark as featured (top 3 products show on homepage)

### Product Filtering (Public View)

Your customers can filter products by:
- **Product Type/Category**: Group by Curtains, Blinds, Shutters, etc.
- **Color**: Filter by available colors
- **Price Range**: Adjust price slider to narrow results
- **Sort Options**:
  - Name (A-Z)
  - Price: Low to High
  - Price: High to Low
  - Newest First

## Page Editor

### Available Pages

Your store includes these standard pages:
- **Home**: Landing page with hero, features, and product showcase
- **Products**: Full product catalog with filtering
- **About**: Your business story and values
- **Contact**: Contact form for customer inquiries

### Editing Pages

1. Navigate to **Edit Pages** from the dashboard
2. Select the page you want to edit
3. Configure page settings:
   - **Page Title**: Display name in navigation
   - **URL Slug**: Custom URL path
   - **SEO Title**: Optimized title for search engines
   - **SEO Description**: Meta description (160 characters)
   - **Published Status**: Show/hide the page

### Page Sections

Add and arrange content sections:
- **Hero Banner**: Large header with headline and CTA
- **Features Grid**: Showcase key benefits
- **Product Showcase**: Display selected products
- **Testimonials**: Customer reviews and feedback
- **Call to Action**: Conversion-focused section
- **Text + Image**: Combined content blocks
- **Contact Form**: Customer inquiry form

Rearrange sections using the **↑** and **↓** buttons.

**Search Engine Optimization**:
- **SEO Title**: Store title for search results (50-60 characters)
- **SEO Description**: Store description for search snippets (150-160 characters)

**Custom Domain**:
- Connect your own domain name (e.g., shop.yoursite.com)
- Contact support for DNS configuration assistance

**Analytics**:
- Google Analytics ID: Track visitor behavior and conversions

### Payments

**Payment Provider**:
- Choose between Stripe or PayPal
- Connect your account in main Settings → Payments
- Currently supports inquiry-based pricing model

## Customer Inquiries

### Inquiry Types

Your store captures four types of inquiries:
1. **Quote Request**: Customer wants pricing for specific products
2. **Product Inquiry**: Questions about product specifications
3. **General Contact**: General questions or feedback
4. **Booking Request**: Schedule consultation or measurement

### Inquiry Management

**Dashboard View**:
- See recent inquiries with customer details
- View inquiry type and timestamp
- Track inquiry status:
  - New
  - Contacted
  - Quoted
  - Converted
  - Closed

**Inquiry Information**:
- Customer name, email, phone
- Inquiry type and message
- Related product (if applicable)
- IP address and user agent (for security)
- Creation timestamp

## Troubleshooting

### Store Not Visible
- Check if store is **Published** in dashboard
- Verify products are marked as **visible**
- Clear browser cache
- Test in incognito/private browsing mode

### Products Not Showing
- Ensure products are marked as **active** in inventory
- Check product visibility settings
- Verify products are assigned to the store
- Review product filters if using public view

### Design Issues
- Clear browser cache after design changes
- Check color contrast for readability
- Test on mobile devices
- Verify custom CSS isn't conflicting

### Inquiry Issues
- Test contact form submission
- Check email notifications are enabled
- Verify inquiry appears in dashboard
- Review inquiry filtering settings

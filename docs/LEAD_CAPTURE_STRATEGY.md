# InterioApp Lead Capture & Conversion Strategy

## Overview
This document outlines the lead capture strategy for interioapp.com to maximize conversions from website visitors to paying customers.

---

## Lead Capture Points

### 1. Primary CTA - Free Trial Signup
**Location:** Hero section, pricing page, CTA sections
**Form Fields:**
- Full Name (required)
- Work Email (required)
- Company Name (required)
- Phone (optional - for high-intent follow-up)

**Value Proposition:**
- 14-day free trial
- No credit card required
- Full access to all features
- Setup in under 5 minutes

### 2. Secondary CTA - Book a Demo
**Location:** Hero section, navigation, feature pages
**Form Fields:**
- Full Name
- Work Email
- Company Name
- Team Size (dropdown: 1, 2-5, 6-10, 10+)
- Current Pain Point (optional dropdown)

**Use Case:** For larger businesses or those wanting personalized walkthrough

### 3. Lead Magnets

#### A. "Ultimate Pricing Grid Template"
**Type:** Downloadable Excel template
**Gate:** Email only
**Description:** Pre-built pricing grid templates for common window treatments
**Target:** Business owners looking to systematize pricing

#### B. "Window Covering Business Growth Guide"
**Type:** PDF ebook (8-10 pages)
**Gate:** Email + Company Name
**Content:**
- Industry benchmarks
- Common profit margin mistakes
- How to increase quote-to-close ratio
- Technology adoption tips

#### C. "ROI Calculator"
**Type:** Interactive tool
**Gate:** Email after calculation
**Description:** Calculate potential time/money savings with InterioApp
**Inputs:** Current quotes/week, average quote time, hourly rate

### 4. Exit Intent Popup
**Trigger:** Mouse movement toward browser close
**Offer:** "Wait! Get 20% off your first 3 months"
**Form:** Email only
**Note:** Use sparingly, test conversion impact

### 5. Blog/Resource Subscription
**Location:** Blog sidebar, end of articles
**Form:** Email only
**Offer:** "Get weekly tips for growing your window covering business"

## Email Sequences

### Sequence 1: Free Trial Onboarding (7 emails over 14 days)

**Day 0 - Welcome Email**
- Subject: "Welcome to InterioApp - Let's get you set up!"
- Content: Login details, quick start guide, support contact
- CTA: "Log in and create your first quote"

**Day 1 - Quick Win**
- Subject: "Create your first quote in 5 minutes"

- CTA: "Try it now"

**Day 5 - Social Proof**

**Day 7 - Check-in**

## Conversion Optimization Tactics

### 1. Social Proof Elements
- Customer count: "Join 200+ businesses"
- Testimonials with photos and company names
- "As featured in" logos (if applicable)
- Trust badges: SSL, data security, money-back guarantee

### 2. Urgency/Scarcity (Use Ethically)
- "Limited time: 20% off first 3 months"
- "14-day free trial ending soon"
- Annual pricing savings prominently displayed

### 3. Risk Reduction
- "No credit card required"
- "30-day money-back guarantee"
- "Cancel anytime"
- "Data export included"

### 4. Personalization
- Segment by business size
- Segment by treatment specialty (blinds vs curtains)
- Geo-targeting for currency and language

### 5. A/B Tests to Run
- Hero headline variations
- CTA button colors and text
- Form field count
- Pricing page layout
- Lead magnet offers

## Analytics & Tracking

### Key Metrics to Track
1. **Traffic Metrics**
   - Unique visitors
   - Traffic sources
   - Page views per session
   - Bounce rate

2. **Conversion Metrics**
   - Trial signups
   - Demo requests
   - Lead magnet downloads
   - Email subscribers

3. **Funnel Metrics**
   - Visitor → Trial conversion rate (target: 3-5%)
   - Trial → Paid conversion rate (target: 15-25%)
   - Demo → Paid conversion rate (target: 30-40%)

4. **Email Metrics**
   - Open rates (target: 25%+)
   - Click rates (target: 3%+)
   - Unsubscribe rates (target: <0.5%)

### Tools Recommended
- **Analytics:** Google Analytics 4, Mixpanel
- **Email:** ConvertKit, ActiveCampaign, or Mailchimp
- **Heatmaps:** Hotjar or Microsoft Clarity
- **A/B Testing:** Google Optimize or VWO
- **CRM:** HubSpot (free tier) or Pipedrive

## Notes for Implementation

1. **GDPR Compliance**
   - Include consent checkbox for marketing emails
   - Link to privacy policy on all forms
   - Allow easy unsubscribe

2. **Mobile Optimization**
   - All forms must work on mobile
   - Use large touch targets for buttons
   - Minimize form fields on mobile

3. **Speed Matters**
   - Forms should submit instantly
   - Redirect to thank you page immediately
   - Send confirmation email within 1 minute
